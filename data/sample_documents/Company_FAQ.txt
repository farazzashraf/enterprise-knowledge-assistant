HR & BENEFITS QUESTIONS
========================================

Q: When is the open enrollment period for health insurance?
A: Open enrollment occurs annually from November 1 to November 30. Changes take effect on January 1 of the following year. New hires can enroll within 30 days of their start date.

Q: How do I update my emergency contact information?
A: Log into the ACME HR Portal at hr.acme-corp.com, navigate to "My Profile" > "Emergency Contacts." You can add up to 3 emergency contacts. Updates take effect immediately.

Q: Can I change my 401(k) contribution at any time?
A: Yes, you can adjust your 401(k) contribution percentage at any time through the benefits portal. Changes take effect in the next pay cycle. Remember, the company matches dollar-for-dollar up to 6% of your base salary.

Q: How do I submit an expense report?
A: Submit expense reports through the ACME Expense Portal at expenses.acme-corp.com. Attach receipts for all expenses over $25. Reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense. Reimbursement is processed within 10 business days of approval.

Q: What holidays does the company observe?

   - Martin Luther King Jr. Day (3rd Monday of January)
   - Presidents' Day (3rd Monday of February)

PRODUCT & TECHNICAL QUESTIONS
========================================

Q: What is the maximum file upload size on AcmeCloud?
A: The default maximum file upload size is 100MB per file. Enterprise tier customers can increase this to 5GB per file. For large datasets, use the AcmeCloud CLI bulk upload feature.

Q: How do I reset my AcmeCloud API key?
A: Go to cloud.acme-corp.com > Settings > API Keys. Click "Regenerate" next to the key you want to reset. The old key is immediately invalidated. Update your applications with the new key before regenerating.

Q: Does AcmeCloud support multi-region deployment?
A: Yes, AcmeCloud supports deployment across 8 regions: US-East, US-West, EU-West, EU-Central, Asia-Pacific (Singapore), Asia-Pacific (Tokyo), South America (São Paulo), and Middle East (Dubai). Enterprise customers can request additional regions.

Q: What programming languages does AcmeCloud support?
A: AcmeCloud natively supports Python, Java, Node.js, Go, Ruby, and .NET. Custom runtimes can be used through Docker containers. Buildpacks are available for automatic detection and configuration.

Q: What is the AcmeCloud uptime SLA?
A: AcmeCloud guarantees 99.9% uptime for Professional and Enterprise tiers. The free and basic tiers have a 99.5% uptime target but no SLA guarantee. See the Customer Policy for SLA credit details.

========================================
SECURITY & COMPLIANCE QUESTIONS
========================================

Q: Is AcmeCloud SOC 2 compliant?
A: Yes, AcmeCloud has achieved SOC 2 Type II certification. Our latest audit report is available to enterprise customers upon request. We also comply with GDPR, HIPAA (with BAA), and ISO 27001.

Q: How is data encrypted on AcmeCloud?
A: All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. Data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3. Customer-managed encryption keys (CMEK) are available for enterprise customers through integration with AWS KMS or HashiCorp Vault.

Q: What is the data backup policy?
A: AcmeCloud performs automated daily backups with 30-day retention for all database services. Point-in-time recovery is available for the last 7 days. Enterprise customers can configure custom backup schedules and extend retention up to 365 days.

Q: What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?
A: Upon cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days in a read-only state, during which you can export it. After 30 days, all data is permanently and irrevocably deleted from our systems. You will receive email reminders at 7 days and 1 day before deletion.
